Simon Game

Instructions

  • In the context of the game, it is essential to follow a predetermined pattern based on the computer's instructions.
  • Each level corresponds to a specific color selection made by the computer, which should be replicated in the correct order.
  • For instance, if the computer chooses red at level 1 and yellow at level 2, the sequence to be reproduced would be red followed by yellow.
  • This pattern continues for subsequent levels, requiring accurate recollection and reproduction of the color sequence.
  • Adhering to this systematic approach is integral to successfully completing each level of the game.
